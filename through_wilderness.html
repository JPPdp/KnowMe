Through Wilderness

A mobile Roguelite game where the player explores the world and fights monsters. With lots of randomness and secrets to discover on each venture.

Project Description:

Roguelite - where too much greediness can be fatal. A colorful world with a cast of characters fighting their way through the infinitely generating biomes with a set of enemies of gods, monsters and creepy creatures. Lots of mysteries to unravel, secrets to discover and levels to explore.

Project Features:

Single player

-Venture the vast world with a bunch of unique characters.

Random Loot & Events

- Random Loot & Events makes every playthrough unique and different

Artifacts and Coins

- Random loot dropped by enemies that are non-beneficial to the player but can be traded to a merchant

Shop

-Random goodies sold by the merchant that helps you progress through the levels, you can also trade your artifacts for beneficial equipments

Curios

-Beneficial loot that helps you on your playthrough

Score

-Obtain your score upon death, tallied by your actions through your playthrough

Database

- Stored in a local database
